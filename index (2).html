Fluency Arena is a community of IPMAT aspirants where we organise English-based meetings and discussions to help members improve their speaking skills, confidence, and clarity of expression. Our aim is to create a supportive space where everyone can practise speaking and share ideas freely. Speaking regularly is one of the most important steps in mastering a language — it naturally improves vocabulary, grammar, and overall understanding.

We conduct a variety of specialised events including debates, group discussions, extempores, and many more exclusive formats — all designed with the single aim of improving your speaking skills and confidence.

Fluency Arena is specifically designed for IPMAT aspirants, irrespective of which year they are appearing for their examination. Whether you are just starting your preparation or in the final stretch, you are welcome here.

The sooner you join, the more benefits you can claim over time. That said, we have also designed a rapid-paced plan for 2026 aspirants so that even they can meaningfully improve their English speaking skills in a short period. Ultimately, the sooner you start, the greater your chances of becoming truly strong at English communication.

Our mentoring team consists of experienced English teachers along with students who have already cleared the IPMAT examination — giving you both expert guidance and real first-hand insights from those who have been through the same journey.

Almost all of our enrolled members report a significant improvement in their communication skills through Fluency Arena. Additionally, we collect personalised feedback from each participant regarding their progress and overall experience, which helps us continuously improve and tailor the programme to individual needs.
